Passengers & Luggage

Vehicle capacity guidance for Toronto car service bookings.

Choosing the right vehicle helps prevent space issues, especially for airport transfers. Passenger and luggage capacity can vary depending on bag size, vehicle availability and trip type.

When booking, include the number of passengers, number of bags, carry-ons, oversized luggage, child seats or special items so Toronto Car Service can recommend the best vehicle.

If your bags exceed the vehicle limit, we recommend upgrading to a larger vehicle type for comfort and safety.

Fleet FAQ

Common questions about our luxury fleet.

What vehicle should I choose for airport transportation?
A luxury sedan is usually best for one or two passengers with light luggage. A luxury SUV is recommended for families, business travelers with multiple bags, groups and passengers who want extra comfort.

Can I request a specific vehicle type?
Yes. You can request a preferred vehicle type when booking. Vehicle availability depends on the date, time, pickup location, schedule and trip requirements.

Do you offer luxury SUVs for corporate and private travel?
Yes. Luxury SUV service is available for airport transfers, corporate transportation, private chauffeur rides, weddings, events and long-distance transportation such as Toronto to Niagara Falls.
