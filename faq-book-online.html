How do I book Toronto Car Service?

You can book by calling (866) 382-7311, emailing info@torontocarservice.ca or submitting the online booking request form on this page.

Do you provide Toronto airport car service?

Yes. We provide airport car service to and from Toronto Pearson International Airport and Billy Bishop Toronto City Airport.

Do you collect credit card numbers on this website?

No. This form does not collect credit card numbers, expiry dates or security codes. Payment details can be handled safely after your request is reviewed.

What details should I include?

Include pickup and drop-off locations, date, time, passengers, luggage, vehicle preference, flight number if applicable and any special requests.

Can I request a specific vehicle?

Yes. You can request a luxury sedan, SUV, executive vehicle or event vehicle. Availability depends on the schedule and booking details.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Toronto, the GTA, Pearson Airport, Billy Bishop Airport, Mississauga, Vaughan, Markham, Oakville, Burlington, Hamilton, Niagara Falls and nearby destinations.

Can I book Toronto to Niagara Falls car service?

Yes. We provide private luxury transfers between Toronto and Niagara Falls, including airport pickups, hotel transfers and Niagara-on-the-Lake trips.

Do you offer corporate car service?

Yes. Toronto Car Service provides executive transportation for corporate travelers, meetings, roadshows, client pickups, conferences and airport transfers.
